About Us

High-quality video shouldn't require expensive software or a film degree. As students ourselves, we know you have ideas but might not have the time to learn complex tools.

CapHacks was built to bridge that gap. We deconstruct popular editing trends and turn them into simple, repeatable hacks for the CapCut community. We’re here to help you stop struggling with the interface and start expressing your creativity.
