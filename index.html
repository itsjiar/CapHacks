Make Your Raw Footage into Viral Hits in Minutes

Learn essential tricks and hidden hacks to make your videos viral-ready using the CapCut editor.

What is CapCut?

CapCut is a powerful video editing app that offers a wide range of features to help you create stunning videos. With its user-friendly interface and extensive library of effects, transitions, and filters, CapCut allows you to unleash your creativity and produce professional-quality videos right from your mobile device.

OmniHuman AI Avatars

Users can turn a simple portrait into a realistic digital presenter. Unlike basic lip-syncing tools, these avatars simulate natural breathing, blinking, and body language to deliver scripts realistically.

Seedance-Powered Video Generation

With the integration of the Seedance 2.0 engine, CapCut now supports high-end text-to-video and image-to-video generation. This allows users to create cinematic clips from scratch with improved lighting, physics, and visual consistency.

AI Clipper (Long Video to Shorts)

Perfect for social media managers, this tool scans long-form content (like podcasts or interviews) to find the most viral-worthy moments. It automatically reframes the footage for vertical viewing and generates synchronized captions.

AI Inpainting & Object Removal

This feature brings high-level retouching to moving images. Users can simply "paint" over distractions—like a stranger in the background or a stray object—and the AI intelligently replaces those pixels across the entire video sequence.

Voice Cloning & Advanced TTS

CapCut’s text-to-speech has evolved into personalized voice synthesis. By analyzing a brief 30-second clip of a user’s voice, the app can generate narration that sounds exactly like them, eliminating the need for constant re-recording.
